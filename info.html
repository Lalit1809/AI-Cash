Platform introduction

AI CASH is an artificial intelligence company with the goal of building fully autonomous AI by unifying the three essential aspects of intelligence, namely Problem Solving, Learning and Memory. AI CASH, was founded by Dr. Richard H. Shinn in Seoul, Korea in 1997 and expanded to Palo Alto, California in 2012.

Human-like AI achieved only when we fully automate the entire process of cognitive reasoning. AI CASH delivers the full autonomy based on its breakthrough technologies: AICoRe (Adaptive Interactive Cognitive Reasoner), a fully autonomous cognitive AI; Memory Graph, AI memory encompassing both episodic and semantic knowledge.
